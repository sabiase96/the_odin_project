Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is an easy, quick, and tasty dinner you
can make during busy weeknights, or for date night.
There are a ton of different spins you can put on
your own tomato sauce. I will be showing you the
most basic recipe that I use when making spaghetti
at home.

Spaghetti can be served along side garlic bread,
homemade or store bought meatballs, and a glass
of red wine.

Ingedients

For this recipe you will need:

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 100mL of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 big can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Spaghetti pasta noodles
  • Salt and peper to taste

Optional

  • Garlic bread
  • Meatballs
  • Hamburger meat

Steps

Prep

  1. Mince 1 yellow onion
  2. Mince 4 garlic cloves
  3. Open can of crushed tomatoes and tomato paste
  4. Brown hamburger meat if making meat sauce

Cooking

  1. Pour olive oil into a pan and set to medium heat
  2. Once oil is shimmering, add onion and garlic
  3. Season onion and garlic with salt and pepper
  4. Stir and simmer garlic and onion on medium-low
    heat until onion no longer has any crunch
  5. While onion and garlic are simmering, add cold
    water to a large pot and bring to boil
  6. Add in desired amount of pasta noodles to
    boiling wated and cook to al-dente
  7. Add crushed tomatos and tomato paste to mixture
    and stir

  8. (Optional)

    • Add browned hamburger meat
  9. Allow to simmer until pasta noodles are done cooking
  10. Enjoy!!
